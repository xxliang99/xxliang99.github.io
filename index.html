About
I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Microelectronics Thrust of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) , advised by Prof. Yuzhe Ma. I received my M.S. degree from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2021, advised by Prof. Xiaomeng Li and B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2020. My research interests are in the cross-disciplinary study of electronic design automation (EDA) and machine learning, including machine learning-aided VLSI design, and design for manufacturability.
